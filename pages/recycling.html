Recycling codes
Recycling Codes are small symbols found on plastic products that help identify the type of plastic to make recycling easier.
They consist of a triangle with a number inside, and each number represents a specific type of plastic.

-PET: Used in water bottles and soda containers.

-HDPE: Found in milk jugs and detergent bottles.

-PVC: Used in pipes and some packaging.

-LDPE: Found in shopping bags and plastic wraps.

-PP: Used in food containers and bottle caps.

-PS: Found in foam cups and trays.
